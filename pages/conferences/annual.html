16th ISDA Annual Conference

April 14–17, 2026 · Vienna, Austria · Marriott Vienna Grand Hotel

Conference Theme

"Analytics at the Edge: AI, Humans, and the Future of Decisions"

Conference Overview

The 16th ISDA Annual Conference brings together over 800 researchers, practitioners, and educators from 45+ countries for four days of intellectual exchange in the heart of Vienna. This flagship event features keynote addresses from global thought leaders, competitive paper sessions, special panels, doctoral workshops, and networking events.

The conference theme, "Analytics at the Edge," reflects the frontier challenges of decision analytics in the era of large language models, real-time data streams, and human-AI collaborative decision making. We welcome submissions spanning foundational methodology, empirical research, and applied case studies.
