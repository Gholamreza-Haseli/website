Member Benefits

ISDA membership opens doors to the global decision analytics community — with exclusive access to research, events, education, and professional development.

Why Join ISDA?

Everything You Get as a Member

📖
Full Access to Journal of Decision Analytics (JDA)

Unlimited online access to all current and archival issues of ISDA's flagship peer-reviewed journal — impact factor 4.8, indexed in SSCI and Scopus. A $380/year value included free with membership.

🎤
Reduced Conference Registration Rates

Members save up to 45% on Annual Conference registration and receive priority access to workshops and doctoral colloquium seats. Student members receive the highest discount.

🏆
Eligibility for All ISDA Awards

Only members in good standing may nominate for or receive ISDA Society Awards, including the prestigious ISDA Fellows Award, Early Career Award, Best Dissertation Award, and more.

🌐
Division Membership & Community

Regular and Student members receive free membership in up to two Research Divisions, accessing focused workshops, networking events, and collaborative research initiatives in their specialization.

🎓
Free Access to All ISDA Online Courses

Unlimited access to ISDA's growing library of online courses in decision analytics — from beginner introductions to advanced methodological workshops — taught by leading scholars from top institutions worldwide.

📡
Free Monthly Webinars

Monthly live webinars featuring ISDA researchers, practitioners, and thought leaders — all sessions recorded and available on-demand in the member library.

📋
Academic Jobs Board (Post for Free)

Members may post academic and industry positions on the ISDA Jobs Board at no cost. Non-members pay $75 per listing. The board reaches 3,200+ qualified decision analytics professionals worldwide.

📰
ISDA Chronicle — Quarterly Newsletter

The Chronicle delivers research highlights, society news, conference previews, member spotlights, and curated reading lists directly to your inbox every quarter.

🗳
Voting Rights in ISDA Elections

Regular and higher-tier members vote in annual Board of Directors elections and referenda on society bylaws — your voice in shaping ISDA's future direction.

🤝
Mentorship & Networking Programs

Access ISDA's formal mentorship matching program, the PhD student mentor network, and regional chapter events — building relationships that advance careers at every stage.
