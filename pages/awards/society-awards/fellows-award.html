ISDA Fellows Award

The highest honor bestowed by the International Society of Decision Analytics.

🏆

About the ISDA Fellows Award

The ISDA Fellows Award is the most prestigious recognition in the International Society of Decision Analytics. Election to fellowship honors long-term, sustained, and distinguished contributions to the field of decision analytics — through research, teaching, practice, and service to the society.

Fellows are elected by the ISDA Board of Directors upon recommendation of the Fellows Selection Committee. The class of new fellows is announced at the annual conference each year and inducted at the Awards Dinner.

Eligibility & Criteria

Nominees must have been ISDA members in good standing for a minimum of five consecutive years. The Fellows Selection Committee evaluates nominees across four dimensions:

1. Research Excellence

Impactful, original contributions to the theory or methodology of decision analytics.

2. Practice Impact

Meaningful application of decision analytics in industry, government, or healthcare.

3. Education & Mentorship

Excellence in teaching and in mentoring the next generation of decision analytics scholars.

4. Society Service

Active, sustained contributions to ISDA through leadership, committee service, or volunteer roles.

Nomination Process

Any current ISDA member in good standing may nominate a colleague. Self-nominations are not accepted. Complete nominations must include:

  • A nomination letter (max 3 pages) describing the nominee's contributions
  • An updated curriculum vitae of the nominee
  • Two supporting letters from ISDA members
  • A list of the nominee's five most significant publications
⏰ 2026 Nomination Deadline: October 31, 2025. Submit your nomination package to awards@isda.org. All submissions are acknowledged within 5 business days.
