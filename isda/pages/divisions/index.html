Research Divisions

ISDA's six thematic divisions unite specialists around shared research interests, hosting workshops, mini-conferences, and collaborative projects.

ℹ
ISDA members may join up to two divisions at no extra cost. Additional division memberships are available for $20/year each. Join or update your membership.

AI & Machine Learning Division

Explores AI-driven decision systems, deep learning for optimization, reinforcement learning, and the integration of foundation models into management decision support. Hosts the ISDA–AI Workshop annually.
