About the Journal

The Journal of Decision Analytics (JDA) is the official publication of the International Society of Decision Analytics. It publishes original research that advances the theory and practice of decision analytics, with emphasis on rigorous methodology combined with significant managerial insights.

JDA serves as a premier intellectual forum at the intersection of data science, operations research, behavioral science, and management — bridging academic scholarship with real-world applications in business, healthcare, government, and beyond.

Aims & Scope

JDA publishes research across the full spectrum of decision analytics including, but not limited to:

✓ Data-driven optimization and machine learning ✓ Stochastic modeling and decision under uncertainty ✓ Behavioral analytics and judgment research ✓ Healthcare and humanitarian operations
✓ Supply chain and logistics analytics ✓ Financial analytics and risk modeling ✓ Sustainability and ESG analytics ✓ AI & algorithmic decision systems
