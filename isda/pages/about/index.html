About ISDA

The International Society of Decision Analytics is a global community advancing rigorous research and practice at the intersection of analytics and decision making.

Who We Are

The International Society of Decision Analytics (ISDA) was founded in 2010 by a group of leading scholars and practitioners who recognized the need for a dedicated professional home for the growing field of decision analytics. What began as a small symposium of 80 researchers has grown into a vibrant global society representing over 3,200 members across 48 countries.

ISDA brings together researchers from operations research, management science, data science, behavioral economics, and artificial intelligence — united by a shared commitment to improving how decisions are made through rigorous quantitative and qualitative analytics.

Our Purposes

The Society exists to:

🔬
Extend and integrate knowledge that contributes to the improved understanding and practice of decision analytics.
📡
Disseminate information on decision analytics to managers, scientists, educators, students, public and private organizations, and governments worldwide.
🌐
Promote improvement of decision analytics and its teaching in manufacturing and service organizations throughout the world.

Organizational Structure

ISDA is governed by an elected Board of Directors and organized into six thematic Divisions. Any 15 or more members in good standing may apply for the creation of a new Division in a particular functional discipline area.

Board of Directors

Elected leadership including President, Vice Presidents, Secretary, and Regional VPs guiding the society's strategic direction.

Research Divisions

Six specialized academic communities enabling focused collaboration, conferences, and publications in sub-fields of decision analytics.

Committees

Standing and ad-hoc committees managing awards, publications, conferences, diversity & inclusion, and strategic initiatives.

Regional Chapters

Geographic chapters in the Americas, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Africa & Middle East expanding ISDA's global reach.
